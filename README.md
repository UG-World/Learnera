# Learnera

Learnera is a structured academic learning platform designed for students from Class 5 to 12 across multiple Indian education boards, along with dedicated preparation resources for JEE and NEET.

The platform is built as a frontend-first educational ecosystem focused on structured notes, exam preparation tools, student productivity utilities, and high-quality SEO-driven academic content.

Learnera is owned and operated by UG World, a digital media holding company managing multiple YouTube channels, websites, and future application platforms. The sole owner of UG World is Utsav Gupta.
